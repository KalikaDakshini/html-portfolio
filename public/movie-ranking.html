Favourite Anime List

My top 3 anime of all time that I hold very dear to my heart

Revolutionary Girl Utena

Anime I describe as "Lesbians fighting against the heteropatriarchy" is a show that uses magical realism and sword duels in a high school graduation setting to present a scathing critique of patriarchy and what one needs to do to liberate from the shackles it puts on us

Dungeon Meshi

Anime about how an autistic man and a lesbian woman save the world from the horrors of taboo and our desires towards it. With a charming and diverse character cast, constant twists and turns in a story with dense worldbuilding, it keeps you surprising and has you thinking with every new episode

Chainsaw Man

I can't describe this, not that it is undescribable, I just haven't given it enough thought. It's shonen Utena to put it simply and the author has the heart of a lesbian.

Princess Mononoke

I love the grim realistic imagery and the environmental vs human exploitation messaging. It is a very unsettling movie in all the good ways
